Idea Origination

The process on most M&A deals is painstakingly manual. While VDR's ushered in a new era in the late '90's, removing the need for physical war rooms (most of the time), they are still today essentially overpriced file sharing sites. Request lists are still kept in Excel. To-do lists are still often kept on paper. Professionals are left with little idea on status without explicit communication, which takes precious time. Things often slip through the cracks.


Smart, Purpose-Built Software
